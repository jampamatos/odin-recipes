Egg Salad

Description:

Egg salad is a great order, either as a sandwich stuff or a side dish for your picknic.

Ingredients:

  • 8 eggs;
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise;
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard;
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion;
  • salt and pepper to taste;
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika;

Steps:

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water and bring water to boil;
  2. Turn off heat and cook eggs in boiling water for 10-12 minutes.
  3. Cool eggs and then peel and chop them.
  4. Place chopped eggs in a bowl, add mayo, mustard, onions and stir.
  5. Season with salt, pepper and paprika and stir some more.
  6. Serve with bread or crackers.
