Broa de Fubá

Description:

"Broa" is a corn flour ("fubá") cake, typical from the Brazillian state of Minas Gerais. Really tasty and texture rich, makes for a great match with butter and a cup of coffee.

Ingredients:

  • 3 whole eggs;
  • 1/2 cup olive oil;
  • 1 cup sugar;
  • 2 cups corn flour (fubá);
  • 1 table spoon baking powder;
  • a pinch of salt;
  • grilled cheese, grated coconut or anise to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven at 180 °C (355 °F);
  2. Grease and flour a large cake pan;
  3. Blend all liquid ingedients;
  4. Slowly add flour and blend as well;
  5. Add the cheese, the coconut and/or the anise and blend;
  6. Add baking powder and blend some more;
  7. Pour the batter into the cake pan;
  8. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes.
